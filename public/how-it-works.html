Getting Started

How ArkitekAI Works

Compare AI models side by side in four simple steps. No switching between tabs, no copy-pasting prompts. Just one interface for all of them.

4 Steps to Better AI Answers

1

Pick Your Models & Mode

Choose which AI models you want to compare — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, or all of them. Then select your mode: General Mode for straightforward comparisons, or Debate Mode for structured multi-perspective analysis with 9 unique roles.

2

Send Your Prompt

Type any question, task, or scenario. ArkitekAI sends your prompt to all selected models simultaneously. Responses stream in real time — you never have to wait for one model to finish before seeing others.

3

Compare & Get Consensus

Read each model's response side by side in a clean column layout. Then check the AI Judge consensus summary, which synthesizes all responses — highlighting agreements, disagreements, and the strongest points from each model.

4

Continue & Save

Ask follow-up questions to go deeper. The AI remembers your full conversation context, so each follow-up gets more relevant. Every conversation is saved automatically to your conversation history.

A Closer Look at Each Step

Here's what's happening behind the scenes when you use ArkitekAI.

🤖 Model Selection

ArkitekAI supports the latest versions of ChatGPT (GPT-4o, GPT-4), Anthropic Claude (Sonnet, Haiku), Google Gemini, and xAI Grok. You can select any combination. Each model has different strengths — some are better at reasoning, others at creativity, others at code. Comparing them reveals those differences instantly.

⚡ Real-time Streaming

Responses don't appear all at once. Each model's output streams in word by word as it's generated, so you can start reading immediately. This makes the experience feel fast, even on complex prompts that generate long responses.

⚖ The AI Judge

After all models respond, a dedicated AI Judge reads every response and produces a structured consensus. It identifies areas of agreement, flags contradictions, evaluates the quality of each argument, and gives you a bottom-line recommendation. This is the most powerful part of ArkitekAI — it saves you from having to read and compare everything yourself.

🔁 Contextual Follow-ups

Unlike using models in separate tabs, ArkitekAI maintains full conversation context across follow-ups. When you ask a follow-up, every model receives the complete conversation history, so responses build on prior answers rather than starting from scratch.

Deep Dive

How Debate Mode Works

Debate Mode transforms a simple prompt into a structured multi-perspective analysis. Here's what happens when you enable it.

A

Roles Are Assigned

Your prompt is sent to 9 distinct analytical roles: Optimist, Skeptic, Neutral Analyst, Practical Realist, Risk Analyst, Long-Term Thinker, Short-Term Thinker, Probability Estimator, and Strategic Advisor. Each role is assigned to one of the selected AI models, giving you a wide range of perspectives.

B

Models Respond in Character

Each model responds from the assigned perspective. The Optimist highlights upsides and opportunities. The Risk Analyst maps out everything that could go wrong. The Probability Estimator assigns likelihoods. You get genuinely diverse viewpoints, not just rephrased versions of the same answer.

C

The AI Judge Synthesizes

Once all 9 perspectives have weighed in, the AI Judge evaluates every argument and produces a consensus summary. It identifies the strongest points, the weakest arguments, and the key trade-offs — giving you a clear, actionable takeaway from a complex discussion.
