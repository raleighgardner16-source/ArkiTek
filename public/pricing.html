Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ArkitekAI pricing and plans.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Pro or Premium at any time, and the change takes effect immediately. You can also downgrade at the end of your current billing period — no penalties, no questions asked.

What happens when I cancel?

If you cancel a paid plan, you'll keep access to all Pro or Premium features until the end of your current billing cycle. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan. Your conversation history and badges are always preserved.

How does usage work?

Usage is measured in tokens — the units AI models use to process text. Every prompt and response consumes tokens. Free accounts get a limited monthly allowance, Pro accounts get 7.5x that amount, and Premium accounts get 25x. You can track your usage in real time from the dashboard.

Is there a free trial for Pro or Premium?

We don't offer a traditional free trial, but the Free plan lets you try every feature of ArkitekAI with limited usage. It's the best way to see if ArkitekAI is right for you before upgrading.

What AI models are included?

Every plan includes access to ChatGPT (GPT-4o, GPT-4), Claude (Sonnet, Haiku), Google Gemini, and Grok. You can use any model in both General and Debate modes. We add new models as they become available.

What are monthly gifts?

Pro and Premium members receive monthly rewards based on their reward tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum). These can include bonus usage credits, exclusive badges, and other perks that change each month.
