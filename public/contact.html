Common Questions

Before reaching out, check if your question is answered below.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot password?" on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Go to Settings in your ArkitekAI dashboard and select "Manage Subscription." You can upgrade immediately or schedule a downgrade for the end of your current billing period. See our Pricing page for plan details.

I'm having trouble with a specific AI model. What should I do?

Occasional model errors can happen due to upstream provider issues. Try refreshing and sending the prompt again. If the problem persists, email support@arkitekai.com with the conversation details and we'll investigate.

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely. We love hearing how people want to use ArkitekAI. Send your feature requests to support@arkitekai.com — we read every one and prioritize based on community demand.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations are private by default and we never sell your data. For full details, read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
