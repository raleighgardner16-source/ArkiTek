Debate Mode

9 Unique Perspectives on Every Question

Debate Mode assigns each AI model a distinct analytical role, turning a simple prompt into a structured discussion with diverse viewpoints. It's the most thorough way to explore complex decisions.

🌞 Optimist

Identifies the best-case outcomes, potential upsides, and reasons for confidence. Looks for opportunity in every scenario.

🔎 Skeptic

Challenges assumptions, flags risks, and asks the hard questions others might overlook. Essential for stress-testing ideas.

📊 Neutral Analyst

Provides a balanced, data-driven assessment without bias. Focuses on facts, evidence, and objective analysis.

🛠 Practical Realist

Focuses on what's actionable right now. Evaluates feasibility, resource constraints, and implementation challenges.

⚠️ Risk Analyst

Maps out everything that could go wrong. Quantifies risks, identifies vulnerabilities, and suggests mitigations.

🔭 Long-Term Thinker

Considers the 5–10 year horizon. Evaluates sustainability, compounding effects, and strategic positioning over time.

⏱ Short-Term Thinker

Focuses on immediate wins, quick results, and near-term outcomes. Prioritizes speed and short-cycle feedback loops.

🎲 Probability Estimator

Assigns likelihood to different outcomes. Breaks down uncertainty into concrete probabilities to guide decision-making.

♟ Strategic Advisor

Thinks about positioning, competitive advantage, and the big-picture strategy. Connects individual decisions to broader goals.

After all 9 perspectives weigh in, the AI Judge synthesizes their arguments into a final consensus summary.
